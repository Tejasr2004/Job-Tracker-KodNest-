A smart, frontend-only Job Notification Tracker designed to simplify the job application process with intelligent matching, status tracking, and daily digest simulations.

### Core Functionality
-   **Intelligent Match Scoring**: Automatically scores jobs (0-100%) based on your preferences (Role, Location, Mode, Experience, Skills).
-   **Daily Digest**: Simulates a personalized daily email update with your top job matches.
-   **Job Status Tracking**: Track your applications with statuses like "Applied", "Rejected", or "Selected".
-   **Persistence**: All data is saved locally in your browser, so you never lose your progress.
